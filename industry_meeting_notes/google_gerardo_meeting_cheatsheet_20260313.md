## Notes From Gerardo

- Data Analyst roles are more basic and closer to understanding how a product is used. This could be one way to get into the company, though it is probably below your strongest lane.
- Data Scientist roles are more complex and involve stronger statistics and machine learning expectations. For many Google Data Scientist roles, PhDs are common or preferred.
- The two role families seem more connected now than they may have been in the past.
- Research Scientist roles are distinct and should be treated separately from Data Scientist roles.
- A possible entry strategy is to consider Data Analyst roles if needed, but this is more of a fallback than the main target.
- Gerardo's rough estimate was that around 70% of your probability is in Data Scientist roles, especially roles that support product teams rather than core research.
- Product Manager could also be an interesting direction because the work is interdisciplinary and your experimentation background could give you an edge.
- For Data Scientist roles, the likely levels to target are L4 or L5.
