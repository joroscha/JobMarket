## 20-Second Positioning

I am an empirical researcher and data scientist with experience in experimentation, causal inference, platform analytics, and creator-facing research. I am not a core model-training or systems person, but I am very interested in AI evaluation and creator workflows.

## What Roblox Is

- Large user-generated content platform with a creator economy
- Users play, socialize, and build immersive experiences
- Creators use Roblox Studio and platform tools to build, publish, and monetize experiences

Useful scale signals:

- 111.8M average daily active users in Q2 2025
- creators earned $923M in 2024

## What We Know About Data Science & Analytics

From Roblox's official job postings:

- The Data Science & Analytics organization is responsible for improving decision-making at scale
- It covers analytical data engineering, product analytics, experimentation, causal inference, statistical modeling, and machine learning
- It is aligned with product verticals and helps discover opportunities, shape product roadmaps, prioritize work, build data products, and measure impact

This suggests DS&A is not a narrow analytics support function. It looks like a broad product science organization embedded with product areas.

## What We Know About Foundation AI

From the official role posting:

- The team's mission is to enable Roblox creators to bring GenAI capabilities to millions of users
- Core emphasis is on safety, responsibility, quality, and efficiency
- The role focuses on evaluation and optimization for user-facing GenAI systems such as text, image, video, 3D, and 4D
- Key work includes evaluation framework design, dataset design, label reliability analysis, LLM-as-a-judge methods, A/B tests, causal inference, metric design, and reproducible evaluation tooling

Interpretation:

- This is not primarily a model-training role
- It seems closer to AI evaluation, product science, applied research, and decision support for creator-facing AI systems

He may not be on the Foundation AI team, but he is likely a good guide to how Roblox thinks about data science roles, expectations, and hiring.

## Main Questions To Ask Matt

1. What do you know about the Foundation AI team specifically?
2. How product-facing is this role versus model-eval infrastructure?
3. More broadly, how are Data Scientist roles structured inside Roblox?
4. Is the Data Science & Analytics organization where most data scientist roles sit?
5. What do hiring managers in Roblox DS roles typically expect from early-career PhD candidates?
6. For this role, do they want someone closer to experimentation / causal inference, or someone closer to GenAI evaluation systems?
7. How much direct GenAI evaluation experience do they expect versus general research rigor and product science?
8. What does the interview process usually look like, and what should I prepare for?
9. What timeline should I expect from referral to interview to final decision?
10. If this role is not the cleanest fit, are there adjacent Roblox DS or economist/scientist roles where my background would be stronger?
